Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second smallest planet in the Solar System, after Mercury. Mars is a terrestrial planet with a thin atmosphere, having surface features reminiscent both of the impact craters of the Moon and the volcanoes, valleys, deserts, and polar ice caps of Earth.
